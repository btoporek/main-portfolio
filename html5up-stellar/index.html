Hi, I'm Brittni Toporek

Educator turned front-end developer in Surprise, Arizona.

After spending the last ten years in education, I have set out to expand my skill set around technology and explore new roads. With a passion for problem-solving, a keen eye for detail, and a love of working with technology I decided to join the community of front-end development. On this journey I have learned a variety of new things including how to work with JavaScript, JQuery, AJAX, React, RESTful APIs, HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, and more. Through this site, I plan to document what I've learned and developed by showcasing new and old projects. Come and check it out!
